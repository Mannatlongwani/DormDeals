About DormDeals

DormDeals is your campus marketplace — built by students, for students.

Our Mission

To make buying and selling pre-loved items on campus as easy and safe as possible. From textbooks to furniture to gadgets — we help students save money, earn extra cash, and reduce waste.

Safety First

We encourage users to meet in public, bring a friend, and verify items before exchanging. Our platform will soon feature a built-in reporting system to keep the community safe.

How We Started

DormDeals started as a small project to solve a simple problem — where do students buy or sell stuff without dealing with random strangers from the internet? Now it’s a growing student-to-student marketplace.
